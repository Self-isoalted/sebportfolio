Mathematics & Fundamentals of Engineering

Derek’s course will be very intensive. Over the course of a year — roughly 40–50 hours in total , assuming one hour per week — we will cover material starting from the very basics and progressing to topics that go beyond A-levels, with many examples connected to mechanical engineering.

Everyone comes into the course with a different level of mathematical background, so it is difficult to prepare materials that perfectly suit everyone. The aim of this page is to provide additional notes, examples, and practice problems to support those who would like a bit more help along the way.

If these notes help even a few people build confidence and make better progress with the material, I will consider that a success.
